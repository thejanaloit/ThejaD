LOLC Internet Banking — supplemental security scan (Phase 1)

Focus on:
- JWT validation in auth-service and BFF proxies (no ALLOW_INSECURE_JWT_DECODE in prod)
- Session cookies: httpOnly, secure in production
- SQL injection in TypeORM raw queries

- Kong route exposure vs direct Nest ports
